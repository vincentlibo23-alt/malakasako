Lamborghini Miura

The Lamborghini Miura (1966–1973) is considered the first true supercar, featuring a revolutionary mid-mounted 3.9L V12 engine and iconic Bertone styling. The 350-385 hp car (P400, S, SV) boasts a 5-speed manual, 177 mph top speed, and a compact 4.36m length. Today, these cars are rare, highly valuable collector items often exceeding $1M–$2M+ at auction.

Price: $2.5 Million
